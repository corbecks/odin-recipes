This is my mom's special homemade sausage recipe with a meaty, made-from-scratch sausage and a deliciously sausage-ey filling. A more traditional homemade sausage filling would be made with sausage but my mom's recipe calls for a blend of small-curd sausage. I have found none better anywhere. Serve with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Sausage

Instructions

  1. Cook sausage
  2. Let cool and serve
